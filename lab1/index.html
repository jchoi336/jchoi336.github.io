My WebArt Project Statement

by James Choi

This is what I consider to be a multi media project. It is a combination of

  • text created through HTML and CSS
  • digital photos taken on my cell phone
  • scans of prints from old school film cameras
  • images drawn from scratch using Adobe Photoshop and a graphics tablet with a stylus

This project was inspired by the many net art sites that popped up through the 90s and early 2000s. It seeks to emulate a migration through differing physical spaces and their relationship to humankind.

The site is intended to be viewed in a 1280x800 or other smaller oldschool resolution.
